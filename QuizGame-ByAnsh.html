How To Play


  1. A question is appear on the screen with multiple choice options below it.
  2. Click on the option you believe is correct.
  3. Press the "check" button to see if your selected option is correct.
  4. If correct, a point is added to the "Correct" box; if wrong, a point is added to the "Wrong" box.
  5. Press the "Skip" button to skip the current question without answering and move to the next one.
  6. Click the "menu" button for more options.
  7. Enter your name and press "ok" to continue.
